## Projects

### NBA Player Value Analysis
BigQuery · SQL · Excel

Analysis of NBA player statistics from 2010 to 2022. Defined a player performance metric and used an Excel Dashboard to show trends based on a player's performance and value relative to their annual earnings, finding that age 23 players offer the highest contract value relative to their salary.

[View Project](https://github.com/gfin5/NBA-Player-Stats-Analysis)

### Medical No-Show Analysis
BigQuery · SQL · Excel

Analysis of over 110,000 appointments at a Brazilian hospital. Used SQL to identify the main factors driving no-shows for appointments, finding that SMS reminders reduced no-shows from 27.5% to 16.7%

[View Project](https://github.com/gfin5/Medical-No-Shows)

### Telco Customer Churn Analysis
BigQuery · SQL · Python

Analysis of over 7000 IBM Telco customers. Used SQL to find the main factors that led to churned customers, finding that signing a customer to a two year contract instead of a month to month contract decreases churn rate from 42.7% to 2.8%

[View Project](https://github.com/gfin5/Telco_Churn_Data)

### Superstore Sales Analysis
Excel · Tableau

Sales from a superstore spanning four years. Used Excel to find what regions, time periods, and products best drive sales, finding that bookcases and tables were negatively profitable products.

[View Project](https://github.com/gfin5/Superstore-Sales-Analysis)
